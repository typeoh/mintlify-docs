# Source: https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives

In this section, you'll find detailed information about Rasa primitives. Each primitive is a foundational component used for structuring conversations within Rasa.

[**🗃️ Flows**\\ \\ Design conversation-driven business logic.](https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives/flows/)

[**📄️ Patterns**\\ \\ Patterns implement Conversation Repair, they're fully customizable and can be used to handle conversations that deviate from the happy path.](https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives/patterns/)

[**🗃️ Responses**\\ \\ Define the messages sent by your assistant.](https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives/responses/)

[**📄️ Slots**\\ \\ Slots are your assistant's memory.](https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives/slots/)

[**🗃️ Actions**\\ \\ Control the behavior of your assistant.](https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives/actions/)

[**📄️ Events**\\ \\ Conversations are represented as sequences of events: user messages, bot responses, and actions' side effects.](https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives/events/)

[**🗃️ NLU**\\ \\ NLU primitives for Calm.](https://rasa.com/docs/reference/primitives/intents-and-entities/)
